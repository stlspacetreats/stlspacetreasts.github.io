About Me

Hi my name is Izzi (they/them) and I have been working in the cannabis industry for a couple years now. I have a degree in computer science with a background in chemistry. I was drawn to cannabis originally for its incredible ability to relieve pain from my chronic migraines. When receiving my first medical marijuana card, I attended an online class about cannabis and how to learn from chemovars what cannabis would be best for you. I was inspired by this class and wanted to make an online platform for others to learn about cannabis. That is why I built the website GreenBee.

After graduating college, I wanted to learn more about recreational cannabis and decided to take a trip to Europe and do what I called my “Weed Study Abroad”. I spent time in Amsterdam learning from coffee shop owners and travelers as well as getting to study at the Cannabis College. The second stent of my trip I spent in Barcelona getting to experience the social clubs in full and learn from local cultivators.

I am continually drawn to cannabis by the seemingly endless possibilities of things to learn about cannabis. I want to continue to expand my knowledge and abilities in this developing industry.
